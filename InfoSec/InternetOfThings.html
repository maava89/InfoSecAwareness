    Smart Devices

    Summary

  • When we refer to IoT then we refer to all devices that are intelligent essentially. IoT stands for Internet of Things and is a broad topic when it comes to smart devices and the technology of the modern world. It can be any technology that is available to the public, from a smart phone to a light bulb controlled by your phone or PC, or your TV for that matter. Essentially all devices connected to the Internet. However, the more technology that emerges every day, the more security issues are there. Ask yourself this: Do you want to enjoy your various devices but at the same time not being exposed to hacking, phishing or any type of exploitation in terms of privacy or simply getting your credit cards robbed for all your money?

    • How?

  • Get a good Anti-virus for your various devices (PC, Smart phone). Preferred one that includes with VPN, Cloud-Storage and a Password Manager.

  • Update your Smart Devices minimum once a month.

  • Have two separate networks to minimize Cyber Attacks, one where you connect your most used devices (Smart TV, Phone, PC). The other where you connect your less important devices (Smart Refrigerator, Light Bulbs, Smart Robot Vacuum Cleaner etc).

  • Change all your passwords regularly. Minimum once a month.

  • Never leave your Smart Devices unattended or open.

    • Why?

  • Better Security at your home or at your workplace.

  • Safety for your devices.

  • Safety for your credit cards.
