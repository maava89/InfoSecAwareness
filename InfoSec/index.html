  • Welcome to our website where we hope you can learn a bit more about what Information Security is, and how to mitigate the risks from the digital world in a successfull manner.

  • What is security awareness?

    • Its the knowledge and attitude one need or possess to protect the physical and especially the informational assets one owns. So to have this knowledge and attitude, one has to understand the physical, technical and operational control measures needed to implement or protect ones self and assets.

  • So what is Information security?

    • It's a set of strategies for managing the processes, tools and policies needed to prevent, detect, document and counter threats to our informational assets (digital and non-digital). Information security should be built around the core aims of the CIA-triad approach. CIA aims at ensuring that sensitive information is only disclosed to authorized persons ( confidentiality), prevent unauthorized modification of the data (integrity) and guarantees that the data can be accessed by authorized persons when requested (availability).

    Information Security Awareness is one amongst many key principles of information security like risk assessment, security design and implementation, responsibility, ethics, democracy, etc. Research shows that the rapidly evolving pace of information system has not been matched with the embrace of security awareness

    Information Security Awareness Group main goals is to make everyone aware that they are prone to cyber threats, change human risk behaviours, beliefs and perceptions and not forgetting understanding and to create or enhance a secure society or environment as a countermeasure to these rapidly evolving threats.
